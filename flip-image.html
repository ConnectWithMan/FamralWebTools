About Flip Image Tool

The Flip Image Tool is a free online utility that allows you to mirror your images. Easily flip photos horizontally (mirror effect) or vertically to correct orientation or create artistic effects.

Why Flip Images?

  • Selfies: Correct mirrored selfies so text appears readable.
  • Artistic: Create symmetrical patterns or interesting visual effects.
  • Correction: Fix images that were scanned or captured upside down.

How to use?

  1. Click Upload Image to select a file from your device.
  2. Use the Flip Controls to mirror the image.
  3. Click Reset if you want to start over.
  4. Preview the result and click Download to save.
