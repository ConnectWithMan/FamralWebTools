The Signature Generator tool is a free online utility that allows you to quickly draw, type, and download your digital signature. Whether you are signing digital documents, PDFs, contracts, or emails, our signature generator provides a seamless and secure experience directly in your browser.

What is an Online Signature Generator?

An online signature generator is a digital tool designed to help you create a personalized electronic signature. Instead of the traditional print-sign-scan process, a signature generator lets you type your name in various cursive fonts or draw it using your mouse, trackpad, or touchscreen.

Why Use Our Signature Generator?

  • Ultimate Convenience: Create and sign digital documents instantly without needing extra hardware like printers or scanners.
  • High Customization: Adjust the thickness and color of your pen to match your unique handwriting style.
  • Type or Draw Options: Type your name using our selection of beautiful cursive fonts or draw it manually for a more authentic feel.
  • High-Quality Downloads: Download a crisp, clear image of your signature with a transparent background, ready to be inserted into any document or platform.
  • 100% Free and Secure: Our signature generator operates entirely within your browser, ensuring your data is never uploaded or saved to our servers.

How to Use the Signature Generator

  1. Type your name in the text field to automatically generate a cursive signature, or...
  2. Use your mouse, stylus, or touchscreen to draw directly in the Signature Box.
  3. Change the Color, Thickness, or Background settings as needed to match your preferences.
  4. Click Clear Canvas if you want to start over and try drawing again.
  5. When you are satisfied with your creation, click Download Signature to save it securely to your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this signature generator really free?
Yes, our signature generator is completely free to use with no hidden fees or watermarks.

Are digital signatures created here legally binding?
While our tool creates an electronic representation of your signature, the legal validity of an electronic signature can depend on the laws of your country and the platform where you use it. For legally binding contracts, you may want to use dedicated e-signature software.

Can I use my downloaded signature in Microsoft Word or PDF documents?
Absolutely! When you download your signature with a transparent background, you can easily insert the image into Word documents, Google Docs, PDFs, and email clients as your official signature.
